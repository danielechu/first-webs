About Us

Empowering Nigeria with Innovative Energy Solutions

At Wenigro Power, we’re more than an energy company – we’re the catalysts driving a sustainable energy revolution across Nigeria. Established with a vision to redefine the country’s energy landscape, we’re dedicated to providing cutting-edge, eco-friendly solutions that empower homes and businesses alike.



Our Mission

We are on a mission to transform how Nigerians access and utilize power. Our goal is to deliver accessible, reliable, and sustainable energy solutions that not only meet but exceed our customers' expectations.


What We Offer


From renewable energy systems harnessing the sun's power to non-renewable solutions ensuring uninterrupted electricity, Wenigro Power offers a diverse portfolio of systems tailored to meet the diverse energy needs of our customers.



Commitment to Sustainability


Driven by our commitment to sustainability, we prioritize eco-friendly practices and renewable energy sources in our solutions. By reducing carbon footprints and promoting energy efficiency, we're dedicated to building a greener, cleaner future for generations to come.
